About Joseph C. Slater
I am chair and a professor of mechanical and materials engineering at Wright State University. I've spent some time working in employer relations, trying to bridge the gap between higher education and employers, and learned about how poorly we understand each other. I continue to perform research, service and teaching, including substantial programming. Those activities dominate this space.
